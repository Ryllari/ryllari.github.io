About me

A proud Brazilian from the northeast of Brazil. Software Engineer, bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering from the Federal Institute of Education, Science and Technology of Ceará (IFCE) and master's student in Computer Science at the Federal Rural University of Semi-Arid (UFERSA) and State University of Rio Grande do Norte (UERN), where I am developing research in artificial intelligence applied to data collection.

Member of PyLadies Brazil, where I was co-organizer of the PyLadies Fortaleza chapter between 2017 and 2020. Big Kahuna of the online edition of the Python Nordeste conference (2020/2021). Member of the Python Brazil community since 2017, having worked locally in the Python User Groups of the states of Ceará (2017-2020) and Rio Grande do Norte (2021-present).

I am enthusiastic about encouraging and supporting the growth of women in technology.
